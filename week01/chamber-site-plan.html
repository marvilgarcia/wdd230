Overview

Purpose

Promote local businesses: The website will serve as a platform to showcase and promote the businesses operating within Santo Domingo, helping them reach a wider audience. Business resources and support: The website will provide valuable resources, such as information on business licenses, permits, regulations, and access to networking events, workshops, and training programs.

Audience

Startups and Small Businesses: Entrepreneurs and small business owners who are just starting or have recently established their businesses can benefit from resources and guidance specific to their needs. They may be interested in information on business registration, legal requirements, and access to mentorship programs.

Scenaries

What upcoming events are organized by the chamber to support business growth in Santo Domingo?
Where can I find information about starting a new business in Santo Domingo, including licenses and permits?
How can I connect with other local business owners and entrepreneurs in Santo Domingo for collaboration and networking?
